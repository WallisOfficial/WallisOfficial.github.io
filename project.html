PROJECTS

PurrPal ·  Sales app for animal shelters



  • PurrPal is a sales application designed for animal shelters. It helps pet lovers easily find the perfect pet by searching and matching based on price, distance, and personal preferences, allowing them to complete the adoption process online.

    • MOCi ·  Responsive trailer web for a movie theatre



  • MOCi is a responsive web page designed for movie theatre trailers. By searching or browsing movies on the page and accessing comprehensive movie information, movie enthusiasts can find suitable films and easily navigate to the ticketing site.

    • Otto Bus ·  Route planning terminal at transit station



  • Otto Bus is a guided route inquiry and planning terminal that assists passengers in navigating public transit in Montreal. With simple operations, it offers route planning and real-time alerts, making it easy for any public transit user, especially elderly individuals with mobility challenges, to reach their destination.

    • MigrateCAN ·  Immigration pathfinder



  • MigrateCAN is both an app and a responsive website that generates the best immigration pathway for potential immigrants based on their personal information. It helps users confirm their eligibility, assess their competitiveness, prepare for the formal application, and track real-time progress afterwards.
